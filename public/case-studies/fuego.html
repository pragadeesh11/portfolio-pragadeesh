FUEGO — Clothing Brand Web App

Duration: 3 weeks   |   Tools: Figma, Photoshop

Overview

Designed a web application and storefront experience for "FUEGO" — a fast-fashion clothing brand focused on aesthetic colors and dark palettes.

Problem Statement

The brand needed a modern e-commerce storefront that communicates lifestyle, highlights product craftsmanship, and reduces friction in discovery.

Process & Solutions

  • Created a brand moodboard and dark-themed design system emphasizing high-contrast CTAs.
  • Introduced product discovery via curated collections and trending picks on the homepage.
  • Streamlined product detail page with size guide, material info and quick add-to-cart actions.

Results

Prototype tests with fashion-interested users showed improved product discovery and stronger brand recall.
