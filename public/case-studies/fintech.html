Fintech App — Onboarding & Quick Payments

Duration: 4 weeks   |   Tools: Figma, Maze, Google Analytics

Overview

Redesigned onboarding and quick payment experience for a mobile-first fintech app to increase KYC completion and reduce friction for peer-to-peer transfers.

Problem Statement

High drop-off during onboarding (KYC) and users found the quick-pay flow confusing when sending funds to saved contacts.

Research

  • Analytics funnel analysis (GA)
  • Usability testing (Maze) with 15 participants
  • Stakeholder interviews (compliance and product)

Key Insights

  1. KYC forms were too long; users wanted progressive disclosure.
  2. People wanted a 'recent contacts' quick action from the home screen.
  3. Confirmation screens lacked context (what balance will remain after transfer).

Design Solutions

  • Split KYC into micro-steps with a progress bar and inline validation.
  • Added a 'Quick Pay' carousel on home with frequent recipients and saved amounts.
  • Improved confirmation UI with contextual balance preview and transaction notes.

Results

  • KYC completion mock improved from 58% → 82% (prototype test)
  • Quick Pay usage increased during the test cohort by 2x

What I Learned

Designing for trust and clarity is essential in fintech; progressive disclosure reduces drop-offs and builds confidence.
